Hallmark.nl

For the course Bespoke Design, we got the opportunity to design a gift experience for Hallmark.nl. We worked in teams of two and each got a user to interview. From there on we could design a personalised digital unwrapping experience, and the tool to create the experience with. At the end of the course a few teams were selected to pitch their concept to Hallmark in an EXPO and we were happy to hear our concept won!

We created an experience to send your love through a recipe. This recipe will trigger a specific memory and feeling of that day you had the recipe together with your loved one. The ingredients would be send to your loved one in a box, on the box a barcode will send you to an online experience to start cooking. I designed the tool to create the recipe and the box with.

To create our prototype, I used Adobe Xd. In a previous course in this first year of the UXD study, I learned how to work with this tool for the first time. Now during the Bespoke Design Project I got to practice more with it.
