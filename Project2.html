C'est Marie

I always enjoy being creative, from making my own things to decorating things to make it my own. The ones I saw on the market were dull and boring so I figured I can make this more fun, colourfull, in the spirit of summer. The people around me asked for one too and that’s when I decided to make it a small shop and C’est Marie was born. Since then I try to expand it little by little every year. I hope to use this name in the future for bigger purposes.
